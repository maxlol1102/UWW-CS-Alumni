## Overview

UWW-CS-Alumni is a project focused on building a centralized alumni network for the University of Wisconsin–Whitewater Computer Science Department, covering graduates from **2013 to present**.

The purpose of this project is to strengthen connections between alumni, students, and faculty by creating a space where career paths, mentorship opportunities, and professional experiences can be shared.

---

## Project Goals

- Connect current students with CS alumni
- Build a stronger professional network within the department
- Track alumni career journeys and industries
- Encourage mentorship and career guidance
- Support internship and job opportunities for students

---

## Alumni Information Ideas

The project may include information such as:

- Full Name
- Graduation Year
- Current Company
- Job Title / Role
- Industry
- Location
- LinkedIn Profile
- Willingness to Mentor Students

---

## Why This Project Matters

Many students want to know:

- Where UWW CS graduates work
- What industries alumni enter
- What roles they pursue after graduation
- Which companies hire UWW students
- How to connect with alumni for advice or mentorship

This project aims to make those connections easier and more accessible.
